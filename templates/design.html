how to get started

The first step is to book a meeting with us via email or through Calendly. This will allow us to get a scope of the project, provide an estimate for the amount of time required to complete it, and to learn about your unique style, all of which is free of charge. Three potential layouts which have been carefully researched to match your requirements are then presented. At this point, it will be up to you to determine the direction of the project! We want to make sure you have a say in the design throughout and are pleased with the end result. This is why all progress is continuously posted online where you can easily provide feedback on the mockup.





graphic design




Outside of photography and making websites, we enjoy working on graphic design projects. We have experience designing posters, album covers, and business cards, but all proposals are welcome! The simple and clean style seen in our websites carries over to our graphic design approach where we aim to engage the audience through creativity, modernism, and easy readability.
