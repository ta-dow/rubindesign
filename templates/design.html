how to get started

The first step is to book a meeting with us via email or through Calendly. This will allow us to get a sense of your project, provide an estimate for the amount of time required to complete it, and to learn about your unique style. This preliminatry consultation is free. Once we have agreed on a project, we will present you with three layouts that have been carefully researched to match your requirements. At this point, it will be up to you to determine the direction of the project. We want to make sure you have a say in the design throughout and are pleased with the end result. This is why all progress is continuously posted online where you can easily provide feedback on the mockup.
